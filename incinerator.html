General Technical Specifications for Incinerators

  • MS epoxy (rust/weather proof) powder coated body,
  • Advance fully automated, Electrically Operated,
  • Smell free burning process, flexible, stretchable aluminum or CI pipe for exhaust upto 3 Mtr.
  • Wall Mountable, Front manual loading for the safety of users
  • Magnetic closing door, smoke leakage proof with dual chamber
  • Easily cleanable ash tray for residue ash collection for the collection of 150 gms or more.
  • Auto cut of relay for over heat protection, Auto power off for Power saving,
  • Ceramic wool insulation for power (shock proof) and thermal safety
  • Burns pads and give less than 1 gm ash
  • User friendly push button with proper usage instruction
  • Power : 1200 Watt / 230V /50Hz
  • Spiral coil with thermal sensors, All accessories used in m/c carry ISI marked or certified
  • 1 year Warranty for all kind of manufacturing defects
  • Graphic sticker display with every machine for easy understanding to user. (optional)
  • Voice Instructions for users in any language (Optional)
