Started in 2010, in the small industrial town Silvassa, at UT of DNH, to market vending machines and incinerators, of other manufacturers. Later in 2018 with the experienced gathered and adopting new technologies, we ventured to own manufacturing, with the brand name of PRIYASAKHI. After a strong research, we came out successfully, with no compliance machines, received by market well.

Complete range of products, for domestic as well industrial models, leading innovation and unique design, with advanced technology updated time to time.
