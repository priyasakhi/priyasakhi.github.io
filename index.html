About us

Arihant creations started in 2010, in the industrial town Silvassa, UT of DNH, to market vending machines and incinerators, of other manufacturers. Later in 2018 with the experienced gathered and adopting new technologies, we ventured to own manufacturing, with the brand name of PRIYASAKHI. After a strong research, we came out successfully, with no compliance machines, received by market well.

We offer complete range of products, for domestic as well industrial models, leading innovation and unique design, with advanced technology updated time to time. We design customer specific machines, to suit their needs, by which, our machines without doubt, preferred by best institutions across country. Indian government also recommending these machines in their SWACH BHARAT & BETI BACHAO BETI PADAO mission and grades been given to the schools, for installation and proper use of these machines.
